Getting started

Home Page: Browse through four restaurants. Click on a restaurant to automatically add an order to your Cart.

Seacrh Page: Search for restaurants by name, dish type, ratings, distance, or price. Click on a restaurant to add an order to your Cart.

Cart Page: View your placed orders with prices. Add delivery instructions. Click "Make Payment" to confirm and see a success message.

Account Page: Check your account details and past orders. Edit your account information if needed. Use "Reorder" to quickly add a previous order to your Cart. Click "Get Help" for assistance via a pop-up message.
